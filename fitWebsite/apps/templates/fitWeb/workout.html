Gym Workout Schedule
Workout Type Frequency (Days per Week) Description
Push-Pull-Legs 3 Focuses on pushing (chest, shoulders, triceps) one day, pulling (back, biceps) another day, and legs on the third.
Full Body 2-3 Trains all major muscle groups in one session. Great for beginners or those short on time.
Upper-Lower 3-4 Trains upper body one day and lower body another. Offers more focus than full body.
Push/Pull/Legs (2x) 3 Focuses on pushing, pulling, and legs, but trains each twice per week with a rest day in between.
Bro Split 4-6 Focuses on individual muscle groups (chest, back, shoulders, legs, biceps, triceps) on separate days. Generally not recommended for beginners.
